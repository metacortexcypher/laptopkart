What is Daas?

Device as a Service (DaaS) is a modern-day subscription
model for device management. It bundles leasing of hardware
devices such as laptops together with support services and
software into a monthly, per-device subscription.

Companies get the right devices, reduced IT Workload, better
asset management, happy employees using latest technology
and overall lower total cost of ownership.
